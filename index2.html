About the program

The program follows the credit hour system.
• The language of instruction is English.
• The student obtains a bachelor's degree in business information systems after completing 129 credit hours according to the following scheme:
• General university courses (24 credit hours)
• Common commerce related courses (54 credit hours)
• Specialization courses (45 credit hours)
• Graduation project (6 - credit hours).
This research falls within the field of specialization.

Vision

To be the leading program in the field of business information systems domestically and regionally.

MISSION

The program strives to provide excellent educational service and to prepare distinguished graduates, who are able to deal with business information systems to meet the needs of the local and international labor market. This is achieved through providing outstanding academic capabilities and stimulating learning environment while adhering to quality and academic accreditation standards

Objectives

The program aims to provide students with a basic knowledge in business processes, in addition to, the applications of information systems to support these processes. It helps students to understand how to use technology to improve business performance and meet the needs of business managers, decision makers, etc.
