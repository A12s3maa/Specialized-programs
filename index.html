Special Programs - Faculty Of Commerce - Tanta University


English Department



About the program

1- The Faculty offers a study program in English which gives a bachelor's degree in commerce in (Accounting - Business Administration - Economics - Statistics and Insurance)
2- Candidates are accepted by the Coordinating office and those with a high school certificate and equivalent, and are accepted through internal coordination.
3- The study is four years divided into eight terms
4- The study is generally in the first, second and third years. Specialization is in the fourth year (Accounting - Business Administration - Economics - Statistics and Insurance).

Program Vision:

The English division at the Faculty seeks to occupy a distinguished position among the corresponding programs in Egypt, regionally and internationally and to approach international standards by providing a distinct environment for teaching, learning and scientific research so that the graduates have opportunities for global, regional and local competition

Program Mission

The Faculty of Commerce - Tanta University offers a Bachelor of Commerce program in the English language, providing a distinguished graduate with academic and professional knowledge and skills in the various accounting sciences fields to meet the requirements of the local and regional labor market in light of an internal quality system based on local quality and accreditation standards.

Objects

Program Goals:
1- Using the scientific approach in thinking
2- Using the scientific approach to solve problems
3- Gain knowledge and skills in all different fields of business to serve the external community.
4- Awareness of global changes and developments in its fields of specialization
5- Commitment to academic, professional and ethical standards
6- Knowledge of laws and related amendments in areas of specialization
7- Preparing project feasibility studies and evaluating investment proposals
8- Ability to employ in various establishments (public sector companies and bodies - investment companies - service companies - banks - insurance companies - stock exchange - petroleum companies ...............)
9- Mastery of entrepreneurship.
10- Education and continuous improvement of knowledge and skills in all different fields of business.
11- Effective communication skills with others
12- The use of information technology in various fields
